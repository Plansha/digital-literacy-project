# Social Media Dos & Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ DOS (Professional Usage):
- Use professional username (firstname.lastname)
- Share academic achievements (hackathon wins, project repos)
- Engage respectfully in technical discussions
- Keep personal profiles private (hostel life separate)
- Cite sources when sharing articles/code snippets

## ❌ DON'TS (Avoid These):
- Never rant about professors/exam results publicly
- Don't geotag hostel rooms or sensitive locations
- Ignore stranger friend requests (recruiter verification first)
- Avoid oversharing health/stress publicly
- No slang/abbreviations in job-related posts
