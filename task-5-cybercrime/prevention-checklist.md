# Stay Safe Online - Student Checklist

## 🔐 GENERAL SAFETY (6 tips)
- [ ] Check HTTPS padlock before entering passwords
- [ ] Never click WhatsApp/Telegram links asking for login
- [ ] Enable 2FA on GitHub, Kaggle, college email
- [ ] Use password manager (Bitwarden) for unique passwords
- [ ] Verify sender email ends in `@vitb.ac.in`
- [ ] Bookmark real sites: vitb.ac.in, cybercrime.gov.in

## 💰 UPI/FINANCIAL SAFETY (2+ tips)
- [ ] UPI PIN never share - even "bank" won't call
- [ ] Screenshot QR codes before scanning (check merchant name)
- [ ] Set UPI daily limit ₹2000 for students

## 🚨 REPORTING
**Immediate Action:** Call **1930** (24x7 Cybercrime Helpline)
**Online:** **cybercrime.gov.in** (file complaint with screenshots)
